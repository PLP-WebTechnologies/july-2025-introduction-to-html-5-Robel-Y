Why HTML5?

HTML5 includes semantic elements like <header>, <main>, <section>, and <footer> that give meaning to the structure of the page. This helps both browsers and users understand the content better.

Accessibility

Helping All Users

Accessibility makes the web usable for people with disabilities. For example, screen readers use headings and ARIA labels to read content out loud.
